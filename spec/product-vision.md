# Product Vision - OptiLens Compare

## Core Purpose
OptiLens Compare is a digital platform that helps consumers identify and compare equivalent contact lens products across different optical retailers. Since retailers often rename identical supplier products to prevent direct price comparisons (e.g., Tchin Tchin naming a CooperVision Biofinity lens "X" while Opticalia names it "Y"), OptiLens Compare serves as a central cross-reference index and comparison engine to ensure customers find the best deals for their exact prescribed lenses.

## Goals
- Establish a high-fidelity cross-reference index mapping OEM supplier models (e.g., CooperVision, Alcon, Bausch + Lomb) to retailer-specific private labels and white-label names.
- Provide a simple, intuitive frontend allowing customers to search for their prescription lenses by either the original supplier name or a specific retailer brand.
- Deliver real-time or periodically updated pricing and package comparisons for equivalent lenses across major retailers.
- Ensure the user interface is mobile-first, allowing users to quickly verify alternatives while browsing in physical retail stores or online.
