# Personas - OptiLens Compare

## 1. The Value-Seeking Lens Wearer (Alex)
- **Role**: Regular consumer who wears prescription contact lenses daily and usually buys them from retail chains (like Tchin Tchin or Opticalia).
- **Needs**: A quick way to search for the brand written on their current retailer box, identify its original OEM model, and compare prices across online/offline alternatives.
- **Pain Points**: Frustrated by the lack of transparency; cannot compare prices because the name on their box is exclusive to their retailer, making them feel locked into buying from the same chain at inflated rates.

## 2. Catalog Manager (Claire)
- **Role**: Data operator who keeps the product database and pricing mappings accurate.
- **Needs**: A robust interface or seed script to populate, match, and modify retailer-specific names and core technical characteristics of lenses.
- **Pain Points**: Inconsistent retailer naming schemes and technical details make mapping error-prone.
