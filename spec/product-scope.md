# Product Scope - OptiLens Compare

## Functional Scope
- **Lens Mapping Catalog**: A comprehensive database and cross-reference index mapping OEM supplier models (e.g., CooperVision, Alcon) to retailer-specific private label names (e.g., Tchin Tchin "X", Opticalia "Y").
- **Search & Matching Engine**: Search endpoint that resolves queries for both original supplier names (e.g., "Biofinity") and retailer-specific brands, returning the exact equivalent matches.
- **Price Comparison Directory**: Retailer price tracking displaying current unit prices, bulk discounts, and shipping fees for matching lens options.
- **User-Facing MVP Dashboard**: A simple mobile-responsive web frontend displaying equivalent lens names, specifications (base curve, diameter, oxygen permeability), and comparison metrics.

## Non-Goals
- In-app checkout or payment processing (users are redirected to the retailer's official website via affiliate or direct links).
- Direct prescription validation (verifying clinical prescriptions with ophthalmologists is out of scope for the MVP).
- Personalized customer profile/account management in the initial MVP.
